Training Equipment

Gym Equipment South Savanna Gym At South Savanna Gym, we provide high-quality, modern gym equipment designed to help you train effectively and safely. Our facility is equipped with a wide range of machines and training tools suitable for beginners, fitness enthusiasts, and professional athletes. Whether your goal is building muscle, improving endurance, losing weight, or increasing strength, our equipment supports every type of workout. Strength Training Equipment Our strength training section is equipped with powerful machines and free weights to help you build muscle and increase power. Available equipment includes: • Bench press stations • Squat racks and power cages • Dumbbells and barbells • Weight plates • Cable machines • Leg press machines These tools allow members to target specific muscle groups and develop overall body strength. Cardio Equipment For those focused on endurance, heart health, and fat burning, our cardio section provides advanced machines designed for effective workouts. Cardio equipment includes: • Treadmills • Exercise bikes • Elliptical machines • Rowing machines • Stair climbers These machines help improve cardiovascular fitness, stamina, and calorie burning. Functional Training Area Our functional training space is designed for dynamic workouts that improve mobility, balance, and real-life strength. Equipment in this area includes: • Kettlebells • Battle ropes • Medicine balls • Resistance bands • Plyometric boxes Functional training helps develop agility, coordination, and core strength. Clean and Safe Training Environment At South Savanna Gym, we prioritize hygiene and safety. Our equipment is regularly maintained and sanitized to ensure members train in a clean and professional fitness environment. Our trainers are also available to guide you on proper equipment use and correct workout techniques.
